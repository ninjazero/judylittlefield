In Loving Memory of Carol Judith Littlefield

Judy was loved by all that knew her. Born in Youngstown Ohio but raised in North Hollywood she graduated North Hollywood High where she met the love of her life, Robert Littlefield. They would go on to get married and raise 2 children between Montebello and Loomis. Educated in early childhood eduction, Judy loved children and would go on to foster and educate young people.

Life 1940-2021
Survived by Her 2 daughters, grand children, and great grand children
Loved Lottery tickets, Orchids, and Elvis
Favorite Color Blue
Favorite Food Cheese cake and ice cream
